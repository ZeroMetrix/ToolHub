About Text Cleaner

The Text Cleaner is a simple but powerful tool that helps you clean up messy text in just a few clicks. Whether you are copying content from the web, cleaning up email text, or preparing input for SEO or coding, this tool ensures your text is neat and ready to use.

Features:

  • Remove unwanted line breaks for continuous text flow.
  • Eliminate extra spaces for clean formatting.
  • Strip out special characters that may cause errors in code or data.
  • Remove HTML tags to get plain text content.
  • Fast, free, and mobile-friendly.
