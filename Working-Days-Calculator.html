About Working Days Calculator

This Working Days Calculator helps you find the number of business days between two dates, excluding weekends. You can also provide optional holidays to exclude them from the count. Ideal for project planning, payroll, or scheduling tasks.

Features:

  • Calculate business days between two dates.
  • Excludes Saturdays and Sundays automatically.
  • Optionally exclude custom holidays.
  • Easy-to-use and responsive interface.
