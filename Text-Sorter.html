About Text Sorter Tool

The Text Sorter tool helps you quickly organize text by sorting lines alphabetically or numerically. Whether you're arranging words, cleaning up a list, or structuring data, this tool makes it easy to get everything in order.

Features:

  • Sorts lines alphabetically in ascending (A → Z) or descending (Z → A) order.
  • Sorts numbers in ascending (0 → 9) or descending (9 → 0) order.
  • Handles large text inputs effortlessly.
  • Great for data cleaning, SEO optimization, or organizing notes.
