About the Roman Numeral Converter

The ToolHub Roman Numeral Converter is a free, easy-to-use utility that converts between Roman numerals and Arabic (decimal) numbers instantly. Whether you're a student learning ancient numeral systems, a developer working with legacy formats, or a hobbyist reading inscriptions, this tool helps you convert quickly and accurately.

Arabic → Roman: Enter an integer between 1 and 3999 and click Convert to get its Roman numeral representation. The converter outputs modern canonical Roman numerals using subtractive notation (for example, 4 → IV, 9 → IX, 40 → XL, 900 → CM).

Roman → Arabic: Enter a Roman numeral (case-insensitive) and click Convert. The converter validates input and returns the corresponding Arabic number if the Roman numeral is valid and within range.

This tool is perfect for:

  • Students studying Roman numerals or history.
  • Developers or data engineers working with legacy formats or specialized numbering.
  • Designers and typographers who need to convert page numbers.
  • Anyone who encounters Roman numerals in books, clocks, or inscriptions.

The converter is implemented to be robust and user-friendly: it offers helpful validation messages, copy controls, and a clean mobile-friendly interface so you can use it anywhere.
