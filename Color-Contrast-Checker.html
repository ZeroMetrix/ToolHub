About Color Contrast Checker

ToolHub's Color Contrast Checker ensures your designs meet **WCAG accessibility standards**. Good color contrast improves readability for people with visual impairments and ensures your website or app is user-friendly.

Features:

  • Instant preview of text with chosen colors.
  • Contrast ratio calculation.
  • Pass/Fail results for WCAG AA and AAA compliance.
  • Helps designers, developers, and content creators ensure accessibility.
