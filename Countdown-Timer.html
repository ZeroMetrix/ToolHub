About Countdown Timer

This Countdown Timer lets you set a custom timer including milliseconds. You can start, pause, or reset the timer. When the countdown reaches zero, you get both a visual and audio notification. Perfect for precision tasks, workouts, cooking, or study sessions.

Features:

  • Set hours, minutes, seconds, and milliseconds for the timer.
  • Start, pause, and reset controls.
  • Visual countdown display including milliseconds.
  • Audio notification when timer ends.
  • Responsive and easy-to-use interface.
