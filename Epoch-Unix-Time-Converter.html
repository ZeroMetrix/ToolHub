About Epoch/Unix Time Converter

Unix/Epoch time is the number of seconds that have elapsed since January 1, 1970 (UTC). This tool allows you to easily convert between Unix time and human-readable date and time format.

Features:

  • Convert Unix time to standard date and time.
  • Convert human-readable date/time to Unix timestamp.
  • Works in UTC to ensure consistency across different time zones.
  • Ideal for developers, data analysts, and anyone working with timestamps.
