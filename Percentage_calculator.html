Percentage Calculator

Formula: (X ÷ 100) × Y = Result
Formula: (X ÷ Y) × 100 = Percentage
Formula: ((New − Original) ÷ Original) × 100 = % Change
Formula Increase: Original = Final ÷ (1 + P ÷ 100)
Formula Decrease: Original = Final ÷ (1 − P ÷ 100)
Popular Examples:
  • 20% of 150 = 30
  • 25 is what % of 100 = 25%
  • 100 → 120 = 20% increase
  • Original before 20% increase to get 120 = 100
